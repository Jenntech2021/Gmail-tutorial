Create a Gmail Account     To create a Gmail Account click on the Gmail Icon ( refer to A.1) top right-hand corner of the Google Homepage.  If you don't have google homepage you can  use any browser and type in www.gmail.com.   This youtube video shows how to create a gmail account for new users.                                         A box should appear click Create Account at the bottom of the box (refer to A.2) Next click the next icon.(A.3)     Fill in the First name, Last name, Username and Password.     Another page will appear that allows you to enter recovery Information. Enter your phone number, recovery email address, and your Birth-date and gender.
